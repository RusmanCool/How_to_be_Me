Who is Sergey Sabirov?           That's me.

        I am a Russian born male and I currently reside in the United State while working on my bachelor's degree in electrical engineering at Virginia Tech. In order to finish my degree by May 2015, I enrolled in Summer I and Summer II terms. Thus, I do not have too much free time. During the summer I also work part-time as a computer technician in one of the computer stores in Blacksburg. I live with my wonderful Russian girlfriend in a cozy house 3 minutes away from VT campus. I am mad about everything relating to technology, engineering which is why I spend hours and hours every summer day on reading and watching news about it. I gain a lot of priceless knowledge about my passion at the expense of going to bed really late. However, I am a night person, so it is not a big deal. Despite being quite busy, I have already done some fun stuff during this hot season. For example, I went to observe the rocket launch by NASA at the Virginia Wallops Flight Facility a few weeks ago.
